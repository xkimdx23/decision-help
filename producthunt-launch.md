## Description (for the "About" section)

Decision Help is a free, positive-bias decision maker that helps you break out of analysis paralysis. No signup required.

Three modes:
• **This or That** — stuck between two options? Get a clear choice with a reason
• **Yes or No** — instant answers to any yes/no question
• **Pick from List** — choose from up to 10 options

Key features:
• Positive-bias algorithm — always get encouraging, constructive suggestions
• Safe content filter — blocks harmful or negative input
• 12 languages with Arabic RTL support
• Dark mode
• No account required — works instantly
• Personalized accounts with email verification & password reset
• Decision history & community browsing

Built with React + Node.js + MongoDB. 100% free, no ads (yet), no data selling. Just a tool to help people make better choices.
