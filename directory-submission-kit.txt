## Short Description (1-2 sentences)
Decision Help is a free online decision maker that gives instant, positive answers. Choose between options, get yes/no answers, pick from a list, or spin the wheel — all with encouraging reasons, in 12 languages, with no signup required.

## Long Description
Decision Help is a free, positive-bias decision maker tool that helps you break analysis paralysis instantly. Four modes — This or That, Yes or No, Pick from List, and Spin the Wheel — give you clear answers with encouraging reasons every time. Features include 12 languages with RTL support, dark mode, a community vote feed, decision history for logged-in users, and a safety filter. No signup required for basic use. Built for anyone who struggles with overthinking, decision fatigue, or second-guessing.

## Key Features
- 4 decision modes: This or That, Yes or No, Pick from List, Spin the Wheel
- Positive-bias algorithm — every answer comes with an encouraging reason
- 12 languages with full RTL support (including Arabic)
- Dark mode for comfortable use at night
- No signup required — use instantly, zero friction
- Community vote feed — see how others decide
- Decision history and profile for registered users
- Safety filter blocks inappropriate content
- Free forever
